Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our roofing services and processes.

How long does a typical roof replacement take?

The duration depends on the size and complexity of your roof. Most residential roof replacements take between 3-7 days. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial assessment.

Do you offer financing options for roofing projects?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options to help make your roofing project more affordable. We can discuss these options during your free consultation.

What roofing materials do you work with?

We work with a wide range of high-quality roofing materials including slate, tile, metal, and asphalt shingles. We'll recommend the best option for your home based on your budget, aesthetic preferences, and local weather conditions.

Are your roofing services guaranteed?

Yes, all our work comes with comprehensive guarantees. We offer a 10-year workmanship warranty on all installations, in addition to manufacturer warranties on materials.

How do I know if my roof needs repair or replacement?

During our free roof inspection, we'll assess the condition of your roof and provide an honest recommendation. Factors we consider include the age of your roof, extent of damage, and overall condition.

Do you handle insurance claims for storm damage?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with insurance companies. We can help document damage, provide detailed estimates, and work directly with your insurance provider to streamline the claims process.
